Grilled Chicken Marinade

Sara found this recipe on Pinterest and it has become one of our summer favorites. We grill this all the time all summer long. It is really easy to make and the earlier you get it marinating in the fridge the better it tastes. It pairs well with asparagus or brussel sprouts.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds chicken breast
  • 1/3 cups olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoons dijon mustard
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons each:
    • Onion powder
    • Garlic powder
    • Salt
  • 1 teaspoon each:
    • Basil
    • Oregano
    • Thyme
    • Paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon each:
    • Pepper
    • Red pepper flakes

Directions

  1. Whisk the marinade ingredients together in a freezer bag
  2. Add chicken to marinade and turn to coat
  3. Marinade at room tempature or refrigerate up to 12 hours
  4. When ready to cook, remove chicken from refrigerator and let rest 15-30 minutes before grilling
