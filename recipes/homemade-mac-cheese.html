Mom's Homemade Mac and Cheese

Bonnie's famous Mac and Cheese dish. Spencer asks Sara to make this for him all the time even though he knows she doesn't love to eat it that much. He often will ask her to make it before she leaves for the weekend while he is staying home.

Anyway to the food portion of this description. This mac and cheese puts Kraft to shame. With bits and pieces of Ritz crackers on the top this special meal is delicious. This is best made for anyone, but kids love it especially.

Ingredients

  • 1 to 1 1/2 cups uncooked macaroni
  • 1/4 cup margarine or butter
  • 1 small onion, chopped (about 1/4 cup)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 8 oz Valveeta sharp american or swiss, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
  • Ritz crackers

Directions

  1. Cook macaroni as directed
  2. Cook and stir in margarine, onion, salt, pepper over medium heat until onion is slightly tender
  3. Blend in flour. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly until mix is smooth and bubbly
  4. Remove from heat.
  5. Stir in milk
  6. Heat to boiling, stirring occasinally
  7. Boil and stir 1 minute, remove from heat
  8. Stir in cheese until melted
  9. Place macaroni in ungreased 9x13 casserole pan
  10. Stir cheese into pasta
  11. Cook uncovered at 375 degrees for 30 minutes
  12. Top with crackers
