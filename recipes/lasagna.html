Lasagna

Description

This is the Lasagna Spencer grew up eating when he was a child. Perfected by Brandi, it's not an Italian staple, but it is some good ole fashioned American lasagna. Lots of cheese and carby noodles go with this recipe.

It is a good meal to have in the fall in the Midwest when you're not worried about your house getting too warmed up from the oven being on at a high tempature. This is also really good leftover. So make it Friday evening to enjoy on football Saturdays and Sundays!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 2 cups cottage cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 pound burger (90/10)
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Directions

  1. Mix egg and cottage cheese
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Grease bottom of 9x13 pan
  4. Brown burger
  5. Mix burger with spaghetti sauce
  6. 2 layers of: 3 noodles, 1/3 sauce, and 1/2 cottage cheese
  7. 1 layer of: 3 noodles, 1/3 sauce
  8. Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes
  9. Remove from oven and add mozzarella cheese
  10. Bake for an additional 20 minutes
  11. Enjoy!
