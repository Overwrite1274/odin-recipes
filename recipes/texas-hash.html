Texas Hash

Texas hash is a meal from Kath that we have all the time. Especially in the fall or winter! It is really hearty and kind of feels like really full tacos. Lots of veggies and carbs in this meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion
  • 1 large green pepper
  • 10 oz can tomatoes and green chilies
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 8 oz can tomato sauce
  • 1 bag southwest frozen rice mix
  • 2 huge and 1 medium huge teaspoon of chili powder. (very descriptive)
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • Tortillas
  • Shredded cheese
  • Salsa
  • Lettuce
  • Sour cream

Directions

  1. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes covered with tinfoil
  2. Uncover and bake 15 more minutes
  3. Serve on tortillas with:
    • Shredded cheese
    • Salsa
    • Lettuce
    • Sour cream
